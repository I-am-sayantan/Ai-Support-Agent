COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

=== REMOTE WORK POLICY ===

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Our company embraces flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance and productivity.

Eligibility:
- All full-time employees with 6+ months tenure are eligible for remote work
- Part-time employees may request remote work on a case-by-case basis
- New hires become eligible after completing probation period

Remote Work Schedule:
- Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- At least 2 days per week must be in-office (typically Tuesday and Thursday)
- Core hours are 10 AM - 3 PM in your local timezone
- You must be available on Slack/Teams during core hours
- Monthly all-hands meetings require in-person attendance

Equipment and Stipends:
- Home office equipment stipend: $500 annually
- Company provides laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse
- Internet reimbursement: Up to $50 per month with receipts
- Ergonomic equipment can be requested through IT department

Security Requirements:
- Use company VPN for all work-related activities
- Enable two-factor authentication on all company accounts
- Keep work devices password-protected
- Do not work from public WiFi networks
- Report any security incidents immediately

Performance Expectations:
- Maintain same productivity levels as in-office work
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during core hours
- Attend all scheduled meetings (virtual or in-person)
- Submit weekly status reports
- Complete assigned tasks on deadline

=== LEAVE POLICY ===

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Annual Leave:
- 20 days per calendar year for full-time employees
- Accrues at 1.67 days per month
- Can be carried over up to 5 days to next year
- Leave requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval required for leaves over 3 consecutive days
- Blackout periods during Q4 fiscal close (December 15-31)

Sick Leave:
- 10 days per year (does not accrue, resets annually)
- No advance notice required for genuine illness
- Medical certificate required for absences over 3 consecutive days
- Can be used for medical appointments
- Family care: Up to 3 days for immediate family illness

Parental Leave:
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid
- Secondary caregiver: 6 weeks fully paid
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Can be split into two periods with manager approval
- Full benefits continue during parental leave

Bereavement Leave:
- Immediate family: 5 days paid
- Extended family: 3 days paid
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request

Public Holidays:
- 12 public holidays per year as per local calendar
- If holiday falls on weekend, following Monday is off
- List published at start of each fiscal year

Leave Application Process:
- Submit through HR portal at least 2 weeks before
- Manager has 48 hours to approve/reject
- HR approval needed for leaves over 10 consecutive days
- Emergency leave can be applied retrospectively within 24 hours

=== CODE OF CONDUCT ===

Our company is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace.

Professional Behavior:
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Communicate professionally in all channels
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Dress appropriately for client meetings
- Be punctual for meetings and deadlines

Diversity and Inclusion:
- Zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, or orientation
- Respect cultural differences and celebrations
- Use inclusive language in all communications
- Report discrimination immediately to HR
- Participate in mandatory diversity training annually

Conflict of Interest:
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest to your manager
- Do not accept gifts over $100 from vendors or clients
- No outside employment that competes with company business
- Family members cannot report directly to each other

Data Privacy:
- Handle customer data according to GDPR/CCPA regulations
- Do not share customer information externally
- Use only approved cloud storage services
- Encrypt sensitive documents
- Complete annual data privacy training

Social Media:
- Do not speak on behalf of company without authorization
- Disclose company affiliation when discussing industry topics
- Do not share confidential company information
- Report social media security threats
- Respect copyright and intellectual property

Violations:
- Minor violations: Verbal warning
- Repeated violations: Written warning and performance improvement plan
- Serious violations: Immediate termination
- All violations documented in employee file
